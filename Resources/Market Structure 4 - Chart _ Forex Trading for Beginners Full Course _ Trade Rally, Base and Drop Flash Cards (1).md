- Q: What are the three basic components of Market Structure discussed in the video?
  A: Rally, Base, and Drop.

- Q: What does a "rally base rally" pattern look like?
  A: A rally moves up to a base, then rallies again after the base (upward move with a consolidation base in between).

- Q: What does a "drop base drop" pattern indicate?
  A: A move down (drop), followed by a base (consolidation), then another drop.

- Q: What does a "drop base rally" pattern describe?
  A: A downward move (drop) to a base, followed by an upward move (rally).

- Q: What does a "rally base drop" pattern describe?
  A: An upward move (rally) to a base, then a drop.

- Q: How can timeframes affect spotting patterns?
  A: Patterns can be identified on multiple timeframes (daily, 4-hour, 15-minute, etc.); a pattern may be clearer on a lower timeframe like 15-minute even if viewed on daily.
